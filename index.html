About Me

I'm a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at Stony Brook University, graduating August 2026.

I design and build NIH R01-funded laparoscopic SFDI/SSOP imaging systems enabling real-time, quantitative optical property extraction in vivo.

I develop CNN and U-Net architectures replacing iterative optical fitting algorithms with real-time performance using PyTorch and TensorFlow.
